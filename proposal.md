# Proposal for a Graduation Planner

**Members:** Alli, Sydney, Katrina

**Description:** This app will be a planner for a student's years at Macalester. They will be able to track their graduation requirements and major requirements, and then decide which semester they would like to take each class. They will also be able to customize the amount of semesters they will take at Mac (if they are graduating early or studying abroad).

**MVP(Minimum Viable Product):** The most basic version of the app would just allow students to plan out their graduation requirements.
